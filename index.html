About Us
Gavathi Shetmal is an initiative by Rasai Agro, Harpavade, led by Mr. Nitin Shivaji Malekar. We are dedicated to bringing authentic, farm-fresh produce directly from the fertile lands of Ajara, Chandgad, and Gadhinglaj (Kolhapur) to your doorstep in Mumbai. Our mission is to bridge the gap between hardworking farmers and health-conscious urban consumers by providing 100% natural and high-quality vegetables, fruits, and grains.
Terms & Conditions
By accessing this website, you agree to comply with our service policies. All products are subject to availability. Prices of fresh produce may fluctuate based on market conditions. We reserve the right to refuse service to anyone for any reason at any time.
Privacy Policy
We collect only essential information (Name, Address, Phone) required for order processing and delivery. Your data is strictly confidential and will never be shared with third parties for marketing purposes. We use secure methods to store and process your information.
Order & Cancellation Policy
Orders: Orders can be placed via our website or WhatsApp. Cancellation: Since we deal with perishable goods (fresh vegetables/fruits), orders once dispatched cannot be cancelled. Modifications: Any changes to the order must be made within 2 hours of placement.
Payment Methods
We accept the following payment methods: UPI: Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm. Cash on Delivery (COD): Available for select locations in Mumbai. Bank Transfer: Details available on request.
Shipping & Delivery
We deliver across Mumbai and suburban areas. Delivery is scheduled on specific days to ensure maximum freshness. Shipping charges may apply based on the order value and delivery location.
